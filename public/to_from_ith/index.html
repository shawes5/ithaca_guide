Navigating To / From Ithaca

Airports

Make sure to book flights at least a couple months in advance, especially if arriving near move-in dates for Cornell and Ithaca College students (August 19-25). Ithaca and Syracuse Airports are the closest options.

Don’t have a car? Not a problem! Make sure to also check out the bus list as there are some available that drive to these airports.

Ithaca Airport

Closest airport for anyone in Ithaca (~15 minute drive)! However, the destinations options are limited so you may need to find another airport.
